Xi'an Cucumbers

Descriptions

Smashed cucumbers in hot chili oil and black vinegar

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Black Vinegar
  • Chili Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • Thai Chilies
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic

    • Steps

    1. Smash the cucumbers with the broad side of a knife
    2. Chop garlic and thai chilies finely
    3. Combine ingredients and allow to chill/marinate in the refrigerator
